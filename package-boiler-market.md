<p><p>The package boiler market features three primary types: A-Type, D-Type, and O-Type, each differing in design and application. A-Type boilers are known for their compact vertical design, making them ideal for limited spaces. D-Type boilers have a distinct horizontal configuration, providing high steam production and efficiency, suitable for industrial applications. O-Type boilers feature a cylindrical design that allows for a smaller footprint while maintaining high efficiency, often used in commercial settings. Each type serves specific operational needs and space requirements.</p></p>
