<p><p>Gas flush bags are specialized packaging solutions designed to extend the shelf life of products by replacing air inside the bags with protective gases. The PE gas flush bags, made from polyethylene, offer moisture resistance and flexibility. PA gas flush bags, constructed from polyamide, provide enhanced strength and barrier properties. EVOH gas flush bags use ethylene vinyl alcohol for superior oxygen barrier capabilities, making them ideal for high-oxygen sensitivity products, thereby ensuring freshness and quality during storage and transportation.</p></p>
