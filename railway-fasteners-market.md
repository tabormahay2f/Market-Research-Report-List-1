<p><p>The railway fasteners market is categorized into two main types: bolt type and non-bolt type fasteners. Bolt type fasteners, such as track bolts and rail clamps, are essential for securing rails to track sleepers, providing stability and safety. Non-bolt type fasteners include items like clips and spikes, which offer alternative methods for fastening rails without traditional bolts. Each type plays a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and efficiency of railway systems, enhancing durability and performance.</p></p>
