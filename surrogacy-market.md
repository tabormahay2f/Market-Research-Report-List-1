<p><strong>The Surrogacy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrauterine insemination (IUI)</li><li>In-vitro fertilization (IVF)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surrogacy market encompasses various assisted reproductive technologies. Intrauterine insemination (IUI) involves placing sperm directly into a woman's uterus to facilitate fertilization, typically used for individuals with fertility issues. In-vitro fertilization (IVF) entails fertilizing an egg outside the body and implanting it into a surrogate's uterus. Other surrogacy methods may include gestational surrogacy, where the surrogate has no genetic link to the child, and traditional surrogacy, where the surrogate uses her own eggs. Each method serves different parental needs and circumstances.</p></p>
