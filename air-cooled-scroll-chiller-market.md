<p><p>The air-cooled scroll chiller market is primarily divided into two types based on compressor configuration: multiple compressor and single compressor systems. Single compressor chillers utilize a single scroll compressor, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for smaller applications. In contrast, multiple compressor chillers feature multiple scroll compressors working in tandem, providing increased efficiency, flexibility, and redundancy. This design is suitable for larger commercial applications where precise temperature control and high demand are critical, enhancing overall system reliability.</p></p>
