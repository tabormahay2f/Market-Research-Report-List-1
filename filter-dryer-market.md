<p><strong>The Filter Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adsorption Filter Dryer</li><li>Refrigeration Filter Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The filter dryer market comprises two primary types: adsorption filter dryers and refrigeration filter dryers. Adsorption filter dryers utilize desiccants to remove moisture from air and gases, making them ideal for applications requiring low humidity levels. Refrigeration filter dryers, on the other hand, leverage refrigeration technology to condense and remove moisture, commonly used in HVAC and refrigeration systems. Both types play a critical role in maintaining system efficiency, enhancing reliability, and prolonging equipment lifespan by preventing corrosion and moisture-related damage.</p></p>
