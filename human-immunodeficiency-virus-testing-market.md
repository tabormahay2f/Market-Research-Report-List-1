<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Human Immunodeficiency Virus Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antibody Tests</li><li>RNA (viral load) Test</li><li>Antibody-antigen Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Human Immunodeficiency Virus Testing market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Antibody Tests** detect HIV antibodies, indicating infection after exposure, typically within 3 to 12 weeks.</p><p>2. **RNA (Viral Load) Tests** measure the actual virus in the blood, useful for early detection and monitoring treatment effectiveness.</p><p>3. **Antibody-Antigen Tests** identify both antibodies and the p24 antigen, providing quicker results, often within 2 to 6 weeks post-exposure. These tests are crucial for effective diagnosis and management of HIV.</p></p>
