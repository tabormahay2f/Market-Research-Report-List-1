<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) market can be categorized into three main types: Single Panel, Double Panel, and Multilayer Panel. Single Panel PCBs consist of one layer of conductive material, making them simple and cost-effective for basic applications. Double Panel PCBs feature two layers, allowing for more complex circuitry and increased functionality. Multilayer Panels consist of three or more layers, enabling high-density circuit routing and advanced features, suitable for sophisticated electronic devices. Each type serves distinct market demands based on complexity and application.</p></p>
