<p><p>The cyclodextrin market encompasses three primary types: alpha-cyclodextrin, beta-cyclodextrin, and gamma-cyclodextrin. Alpha-cyclodextrin, with six glucose units, is utilized for its low-calorie properties in food applications. Beta-cyclodextrin, containing seven glucose units, is widely used in pharmaceuticals for enhancing solubility and stability. Gamma-cyclodextrin, comprising eight glucose units, possesses a larger cavity suitable for various applications in cosmetics and agriculture. Cyclodextrin derivatives, modified forms of these basic types, further expand functionality across multiple sectors, enhancing their versatility.</p></p>
