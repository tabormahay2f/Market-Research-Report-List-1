<p><strong>The Anti Fog Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophilic Anti-fog</li><li>Hydrophobic Anti-fog</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-fog paint market is primarily classified into hydrophilic and hydrophobic types. Hydrophilic anti-fog paints attract moisture, forming a thin film that prevents fogging on surfaces, making them ideal for applications in cooler, humid environments. In contrast, hydrophobic anti-fog paints repel water, causing droplets to bead and roll off, which works effectively in warmer settings where condensation is a concern. Both types enhance visibility and safety in various industries, including automotive, eyewear, and other optical applications.</p></p>
