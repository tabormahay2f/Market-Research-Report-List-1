<p><p>Attapulgite clay is primarily categorized into two market types: colloidal and sorptive. Colloidal attapulgite is characterized by its fine particle size and high surface area, making it ideal for applications such as drilling fluids, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals. Sorptive attapulgite, on the other hand, excels in its ability to absorb liquids and gases, widely used in environmental cleanup, cat litter, and agricultural sectors. Both types leverage the unique properties of attapulgite for diverse industrial applications, highlighting its versatility.</p></p>
