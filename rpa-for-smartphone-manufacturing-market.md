<p><p>Robotic Process Automation (RPA) in smartphone manufacturing involves various robotic types tailored for specific tasks. Cartesian robots use linear movements for assembly tasks, ensuring precision and speed. SCARA (Selective Compliance Assembly Robot Arm) robots excel in tasks requiring horizontal movement and flexibility, ideal for assembling components. Other types may include collaborative robots (cobots) designed to work alongside human operators for tasks requiring dexterity and adaptability. Together, these robotic systems enhance efficiency, reduce production costs, and improve product quality in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
