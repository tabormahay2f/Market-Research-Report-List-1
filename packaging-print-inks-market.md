<p><ul><li>Lithographic Printing</li><li>Gravure Printing</li><li>Flexographic Printing</li><li>Digital Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Packaging Print Inks market encompasses various printing types, each suited for different applications. Lithographic printing uses a flat surface to transfer ink, making it ideal for high-quality graphics. Gravure printing employs engraved cylinders, producing vibrant colors for long runs, often used in flexible packaging. Flexographic printing utilizes elastic plates, suitable for a variety of substrates, particularly in labels and cartons. Digital printing allows for quick, on-demand production, ideal for short runs and customization, enhancing versatility in packaging solutions.</p></p>
