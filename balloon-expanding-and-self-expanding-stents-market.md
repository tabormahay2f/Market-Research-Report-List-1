<p><p>The balloon expanding and self-expanding stents markets are segments of the broader cardiovascular device industry. Balloon expanding stents are deployed via a catheter and expanded using a balloon, providing rigid support to keep arteries open. In contrast, self-expanding stents are made of materials that automatically expand upon deployment, adapting to vessel size changes. Both types are crucial in treating conditions like atherosclerosis, with distinct applications based on the required support level and the vessel's characteristics.</p></p>
