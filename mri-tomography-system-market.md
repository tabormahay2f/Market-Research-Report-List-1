<p><strong>The MRI Tomography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Field</li><li>Midfield</li><li>High Field</li><li>Ultra-High Field</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI tomography system market is segmented into four types based on magnetic field strength. Low-field MRI systems typically operate below 1.0 Tesla and are suited for certain applications like extremity imaging. Mid-field MRI, ranging from 1.0 to 3.0 Tesla, offers improved resolution and is widely utilized in clinical settings. High-field MRI, at 3.0 Tesla and above, provides superior imaging quality, particularly for neurological studies. Ultra-high field MRI, exceeding 7.0 Tesla, is primarily used for advanced research applications, enabling unprecedented detail in imaging.</p></p>
