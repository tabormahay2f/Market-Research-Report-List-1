<p><p>Pouch cells are a type of lithium-ion battery known for their lightweight, flexible design and high energy density. Their design enables better heat dissipation and increased efficiency.</p><p>The Pouch Cells Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period, driven by rising demand for electric vehicles and portable electronic devices.
