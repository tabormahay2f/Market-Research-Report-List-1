<p><p>The Electrically Heated Catalytic Converter (EHCC) market includes primarily two types: two-way and three-way converters, along with other variations. Two-way converters focus on oxidizing carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons, making them suitable for simpler applications. In contrast, three-way converters manage oxidation reactions as well as the reduction of nitrogen oxides, ensuring comprehensive emissions control.
