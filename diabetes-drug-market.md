<p><strong>The Diabetes Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Insulin</li><li>Sensitizers</li><li>SGLT-2 Inhibitors</li><li>Alpha-glucosidase Inhibitors</li><li>Secretagogues</li><li>Peptide Analogs</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diabetes drug market comprises various categories. Insulin facilitates glucose regulation in Type 1 and advanced Type 2 diabetes. Sensitizers, like metformin, enhance the body's response to insulin. SGLT-2 inhibitors reduce glucose reabsorption in kidneys, while alpha-glucosidase inhibitors hinder carbohydrate absorption in the intestines. Secretagogues stimulate insulin secretion from the pancreas. Peptide analogs mimic hormone functions to regulate blood sugar. Other therapeutic options include combination therapies and newer agents, addressing diverse patient needs in diabetes management.</p></p>
