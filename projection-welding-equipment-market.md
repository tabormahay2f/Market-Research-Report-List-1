<p><p>Projection welding equipment is categorized into three main types: Manual Welding Machines, Semi-Automatic Welding Machines, and Automatic Welding Machines. Manual welding machines require operators to position and activate the welding process, offering flexibility for small-scale applications. Semi-automatic machines enhance productivity by automating certain tasks while still requiring operator involvement. Automatic welding machines are fully automated systems designed for high-volume production, ensuring consistency and efficiency in welding processes, making them ideal for large-scale manufacturing environments.</p></p>
