<p><strong>The Industrial Wireless in Process Industries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN)</li><li>Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN)</li><li>Satellite (GNSS)</li><li>Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN)</li><li>Cellular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial wireless in process industries encompasses various market types for communication and data exchange. Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN) offers high-speed connectivity within limited areas, ideal for monitoring and control applications. Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN) enables short-range communication for devices, facilitating automation. Satellite (GNSS) provides global coverage, supporting remote operations. Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN) connects IoT devices over long distances with minimal power usage. Cellular networks deliver robust mobile connectivity, enhancing real-time data transmission in industrial environments.</p></p>
