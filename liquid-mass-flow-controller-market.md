<p><ul><li>"Pressure Differential Type"</li><li>"Thermal Type"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid mass flow controller market is segmented into two main types: pressure differential and thermal. Pressure differential types operate by measuring the difference in pressure across a flow restrictor, allowing precise control of liquid flow rates. In contrast, thermal types utilize heat transfer principles, measuring the temperature changes caused by fluid flow to determine mass flow rates. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, chemical processing, and semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring accurate and reliable liquid handling processes.</p></p>
