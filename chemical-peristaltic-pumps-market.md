<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Mechanical</li><li>Solenoid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical peristaltic pumps market comprises three primary types. Hydraulic peristaltic pumps utilize fluid pressure to drive motion, offering efficient fluid transfer with minimal shear stress. Mechanical peristaltic pumps rely on rotating elements to compress tubing, ensuring precise flow control for various applications. Solenoid peristaltic pumps employ electromagnetic forces to create pumping actions, providing quick and reliable operation for smaller flows. Each type serves distinct needs in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and water treatment, highlighting their versatility and reliability.</p></p>
