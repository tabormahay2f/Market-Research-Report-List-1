<p><strong>The Mycology Immunoassays Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ELISA</li><li>Rapid Tests</li><li>ELISPOT</li><li>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mycology immunoassays testing market includes various techniques to detect fungal infections. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) measures antibody or antigen levels for diagnostic purposes. Rapid tests provide quick results, enhancing clinical decision-making. ELISPOT detects specific immune responses by quantifying cytokine-secreting cells. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies fungal DNA, enabling precise identification of pathogens. Each method offers unique advantages, such as sensitivity, speed, and specificity, catering to diverse diagnostic needs in mycology and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
