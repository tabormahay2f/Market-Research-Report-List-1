<p><p>The vehicle engine accessories market includes critical components like pressure sensors, temperature sensors, position sensors, flow sensors, and humidifiers. Position sensors track the location of various engine components, enhancing control and efficiency. Flow sensors measure fluid movement, aiding in fuel and air management. Humidifiers, though less common, maintain optimal humidity levels within the engine for improved performance and longevity.</p></p>
