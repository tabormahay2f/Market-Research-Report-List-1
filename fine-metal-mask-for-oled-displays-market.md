<p><p>The Fine Metal Mask (FMM) market for OLED displays encompasses various fabrication methods, including etching, electroforming, and multi-material composite techniques. The etching method involves precise patterning of thin metal layers to create fine openings for material deposition. Electroforming utilizes electroplating to create highly accurate masks, enabling improved resolution and uniformity. The multi-material composite method combines different materials to enhance mask performance, offering flexibility and durability. Each technique contributes uniquely to the efficiency and quality of OLED display manufacturing.</p></p>
