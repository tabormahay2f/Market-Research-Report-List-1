<p><p>General Motion Control (GMC) involves managing the movement and position of machinery in automation systems. The market is categorized into three primary types: PLC-Based systems use programmable logic controllers for motion control tasks, offering flexibility and integration with other automation processes. PC-Based systems leverage personal computers for more complex motion tasks, providing enhanced processing power and user interfaces. Stand-Alone systems are dedicated units designed specifically for motion control applications, offering simplicity and ease of installation without requiring external computing resources.</p></p>
