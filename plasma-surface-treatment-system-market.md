<p><p>The Plasma Surface Treatment System market is categorized into two primary types: Atmospheric Pressure Type and Low Pressure/Vacuum Plasma Type. Atmospheric Pressure Plasma treatments occur at ambient pressure, making them suitable for high-speed applications and offering versatility in surface modification without the need for vacuum systems. Conversely, Low Pressure or Vacuum Plasma treatments operate in controlled environments, allowing for precise and uniform surface modifications, ideal for complex geometries and delicate materials, enhancing adhesion, wettability, and cleanliness.</p></p>
