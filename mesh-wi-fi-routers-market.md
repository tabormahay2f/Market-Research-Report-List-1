<p><p>The Mesh Wi-Fi routers market is categorized into Bi-Band, Tri-Band, and Quad-Band types based on frequency channels. Bi-Band routers operate on two frequencies—2.4 GHz and 5 GHz—providing decent performance for basic tasks. Tri-Band routers add an additional 5 GHz band, enhancing performance in environments with numerous devices. Quad-Band routers further include an additional 2.4 GHz or higher frequency, delivering optimized speeds for high-demand tasks and better handling of multiple connections, ideal for smart homes or offices.</p></p>
