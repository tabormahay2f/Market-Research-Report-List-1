<p><p>Short fiber reinforced thermoplastic composites are categorized based on the matrix material used. Polyamide (PA) offers strong durability and impact resistance, making it suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Polypropylene (PP) is lightweight and cost-effective, popular for consumer goods and packaging. Polybutylene Terephthalate (PBT) provides excellent electrical properties and heat resistance, ideal for electronic components. Other materials may include polycarbonate, acrylonitrile butadiene styrene (ABS), and polyethylene, each serving specialized applications across various industries.</p></p>
