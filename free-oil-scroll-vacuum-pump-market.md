<p><p>The Free-oil Scroll Vacuum Pump market is categorized into two main types: single-sided scroll and double-sided scroll pumps. Single-sided scroll pumps utilize one spiral scroll to create a vacuum, making them compact and efficient for smaller applications. In contrast, double-sided scroll pumps feature two interleaved spirals, enabling higher pumping speeds and greater capacity, making them suitable for larger industrial applications. Both types are designed to operate without oil, ensuring cleaner operation and minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
