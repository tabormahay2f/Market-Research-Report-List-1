<p><p>Centrifugal air blowers are categorized into high pressure, medium pressure, and low pressure based on their operating pressure ranges. High-pressure blowers operate typically above 15 psi and are used in applications requiring powerful airflow, such as pneumatic conveying and industrial processes. Medium-pressure blowers operate between 5 to 15 psi, suitable for applications like wastewater treatment and material handling. Low-pressure blowers, operating below 5 psi, serve applications such as aquaculture aeration and cooling systems, providing efficient airflow at lower energy costs.</p></p>
