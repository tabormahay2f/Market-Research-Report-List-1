<p><p>The functional coil coating market comprises three main types: primer, back paint, and topcoat. Primer serves as the foundational layer, ensuring strong adhesion and corrosion resistance on metal substrates. Back paint, typically applied to the reverse side of metal sheets, enhances protection against weather and chemical exposure. Topcoat offers aesthetic and functional properties, providing color, matte or glossy finishes, and additional durability features like UV resistance. Together, these coatings create a comprehensive protective solution for various applications.</p></p>
