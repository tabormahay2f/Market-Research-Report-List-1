<p><p>Money insurance encompasses various market types, primarily focusing on protecting cash assets during transit and while on premises. Money in Transit insurance covers losses from theft, robbery, or damage to cash and valuables while being transported between locations. In contrast, Money on Premises insurance safeguards against risks like theft, vandalism, or natural disasters affecting cash stored at a business location. Together, these types provide essential coverage, ensuring businesses manage risks associated with handling monetary assets effectively.</p></p>
